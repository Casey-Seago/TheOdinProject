Lasagna

Description

Lasagna is a dish made up of layers of ricotta, mozzarrella, noodles, and tomato sauce.

It is an amazing dish!

Ingredients

  • Mozzarella
  • Ricotta, Whole milk only
  • Tomato Sauce
  • Lasagna noodles
  • Hearty appetite or friends to share it with
  1. Layer sauce, noodles, then cheese. Repeat 3-4 times
  2. Bake for time at a temp
  3. Bon appetit!
